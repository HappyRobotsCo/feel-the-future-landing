A Live Demo Experience

Feel the Future

Three AI agents will work on your real data simultaneously. One builds you a website. One triages your email. One organizes your files. All in about 15 minutes, right on your Mac.

What will happen

Three agents, three missions

Once you run the setup command, a dashboard opens in your browser. You enter your LinkedIn URL, click Launch, and watch three AI agents go to work.

Mission 01

Research & Build

An AI agent researches your LinkedIn profile and builds you a personal website from scratch. You'll see it write real code with your name, your career, your skills.

Mission 02

Email Triage

An AI agent reads your recent Gmail messages, categorizes them by urgency, and drafts replies for the important ones. Read-only — nothing is ever sent.

Mission 03

Organize Documents

An AI agent scans a folder on your Mac, intelligently sorts your files into categories, and generates an undo script so everything is fully reversible.

Get started

Run one command

The setup takes about 60 seconds. It installs everything you need, opens a dashboard in your browser, and you're ready to launch.

1

Open Terminal

Press Cmd + Space to open Spotlight, type Terminal, and hit Enter.

2

Paste this command and hit Enter

curl -sL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HappyRobotsCo/feel-the-agi-app/main/setup.sh | bash

The script will walk you through each step with friendly progress messages.

3

Follow the prompts

You'll sign in to your Anthropic account and connect your Gmail. The dashboard opens automatically when setup is complete. Enter your LinkedIn URL, click Launch, and watch the future unfold.
